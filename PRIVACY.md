# Privacy Policy — FRMM Shift Light Controller

_Last updated: May 2026_

## Data Collection

FRMM Shift Light Controller does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What the app does

- Reads UDP telemetry packets sent locally by Forza Horizon 5/6 on your PC
- Sends RPM LED commands to your Logitech G923 wheel via USB (HID)
- Saves your settings (port number, theme, language, LED thresholds) to a local `config.json` file on your device

## Internet access

The app does not connect to the internet. The only exception is the optional **Donate** button, which opens your default web browser to `paypal.me/RichardPM20` when you click it. No data is sent automatically.

## Third-party services

The app communicates locally with **Logitech G HUB** (if running) via a local WebSocket connection on `localhost`. No data leaves your device.
